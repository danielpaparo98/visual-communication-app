Making visual communication charts easy!

Easily create visual communication (PECS) charts for adults and children with difficulties communicating due to sickness or disability that allow for quick and concise communication with family members and medical staff – for free!

I created this web app and made it freely available to hopefully help others who may be in the same situation as my family was a couple of years ago. When my uncle became unable to verbally communicate due to his declining health, we decided to create a set of communication charts so he could quickly and concisely talk to his family and medical staff. When I searched online for a visual communication chart creator, I learnt that there were no free or easy apps for creating them. Instead, I spent the best part of an afternoon sourcing icons and designing a chart that would be easily readable for all parties. The charts were used by medical staff who commented on how useful they were for communicating with my uncle.

I believe that the vast majority of people in this same situation would not have the time nor effort available to manually create these useful visual communication charts. I made this app in the hopes that others would be able to create communication charts quickly and easily – and allow those having difficulties communicating verbally, to communicate visually.

I am still actively developing this application in my free time. If there are any issues you run into or you have a suggestion for improving the app, please feel free to get in contact and I’ll see how I can help!
